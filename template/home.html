Mapping Out Essential College Data for New Students

This project aims to gather and organize essential information about various aspects of college life into a single, accessible resource for students. It focuses on collecting data from academic departments, campus services, student organizations, and key campus events to create a comprehensive guide that will help students navigate their college journey more effectively.
